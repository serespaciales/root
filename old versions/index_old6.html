This is a modular graphics garden that grows on the internet. Draw on modules and watch your visuals change over time. Each poster grows with the water, sun, and vitamins you give it. You can see and download both the original and the updated versions, based on a _seed_ logic.

root is free & open-source. The repository will be public soon at github.com/serespaciales/root.

How to Start

  • *Press H to enter the canvas, press H again to return here.
  • *Select a tool (gradient or shape).
  • *Draw or click on modules to add visuals.
  • *Use water, sun or vitamins to grow and transform your creation.
  • *Save, export or share any stage using your unique seed.
  • *No previous experience required. Just explore and experiment!

You can use root to:

  • *Create and Share Visuals inspired by generative art and modular systems.
  • *Develop simple or complex visual compositions in a flexible, non-destructive environment.
  • *Model scenarios of visual evolution through algorithmic growth simulations.
  • *Explore new pipelines in design projects.
  • *Have fun :)

FAQ

  • *What is root?
    A modular visual design tool where your compositions grow and evolve online.
  • *Do I need to know design or programming?
    No. Root is made for everyone—just pick a tool and start creating.
  • *How does growth work?
    Each visual grows and transforms based on your actions and settings (water, sun, vitamins), thanks to algorithmic simulations that let you see every stage.
  • *Can I download or share my creations?
    Yes, you can save and download any version. Share your unique seed so others can remix your work.
  • *What can I create in root?
    From generative art and branding to layouts, growth simulations, and more.
  • *Is root free?
    Yes, root is free for all users.
  • *How are visuals saved?
    Each creation starts from a unique seed, which allows you to revisit or share your work anytime.
  • *Is collaboration possible?
    You can share your seed so others can collaborate. Real-time collaboration is coming soon.
